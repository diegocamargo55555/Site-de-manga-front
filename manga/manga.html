Everyone Loves Her

With the sudden acquisition of the ability, Kang Mina’s life is completely turned upside down. Now, she stands at a crossroads where a single choice can lead to irreversible consequences. Amidst anger, confusion, and a suppressed sense of justice, Mina struggles with how to use her newfound power as she embarks on a new journey. Where will her power take her?
